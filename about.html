About TEACH

We apply a Catapult (manati) Approach to creating hands-on solutions that merge technology with a sustainability ethos.

Our Mission

Facilitating sustainable use of natural resources by investing in environmentally friendly projects and acknowledging environmental justice. [3] The main goal of the organization is to see an improved livelihood of people through sustainable exploitation of the available productive resources. [3]

Our Vision

Tanzania Eco-Tech and Conservation Hub (TEACH) is a non-profit conservation organization invested in nature conservation, ecotourism, environmental justice, sustainable engineering, and community empowerment. [3] Our vision is to ensure a sustained balance of nature. [3]

"JEDI (Justice, Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion), sustainability, transparency, and integrity."

Our Core Values

TEACH believes in JEDI (justice, equity, diversity and inclusion), sustainability, transparency, and integrity as core values that guide every aspect of our work, from community engagement to project implementation.
