Education & Training

TEACH’s Education & Training Program focuses on promoting Nature-based Solutions (NbS) by empowering communities with practical, sustainable knowledge rooted in local challenges and opportunities. Through climate-smart agricultural practices and eco-innovation, we help build resilience and self-reliance.

🌱 Key Training Areas

  • Design and implementation of small space gardens for urban and peri-urban homes
  • Use of solar-powered water pumps to reduce dependency on grid electricity and fuel
  • Drip irrigation systems to conserve water and improve crop productivity
  • Creation and application of biochar to enrich soil health and sequester carbon
  • Promotion of agroecology and agroforestry to enhance biodiversity and long-term farm resilience

📚 Community Engagement

TEACH reaches communities through:

  • Interactive workshops and demonstrations at the TEACH Center
  • Participation in exhibitions and multi-stakeholder partnerships
  • On-site capacity-building sessions tailored to local farmer and youth groups
“Think creatively and sustainably within STEM fields”
— Dauson Msumange, Founder & Director of TEACH

Our mission is to transfer practical, science-based skills that communities can adopt and adapt for local resilience. Every training session promotes circular thinking, climate adaptation, and sustainable land use practices.
