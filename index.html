About TEACH

We apply Catapult (manati) Approach to creating hand-on solutions that merge technology with sustainability ethos

Our Mission

Facilitating sustainable use of natural resources by investing in environmentally friendly projects and acknowledging environmental justice.

The main goal of the organization is to see an improved livelihood of people through sustainable exploitation of the available productive resources.

Values

TEACH believes in JEDI (justice, equity, diversity and inclusion), sustainability, transparency, and integrity as core values.
