Who We Are

Founded in 2020, TEACH is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to fostering a sustainable and equitable future for Tanzania. We believe in the power of local communities to drive change from the ground up.

Our multidisciplinary team of conservationists, engineers, and educators work hand-in-hand with local partners to implement innovative solutions that address critical environmental challenges while creating economic opportunities.

What We Do

Community-Led Conservation

We empower local communities to protect and restore their natural resources through training, resource provision, and the establishment of community-managed conservation areas.

Eco-Tech Innovation

We develop and deploy appropriate technologies for clean energy, sustainable agriculture, and water purification, tailored to the specific needs of the communities we serve.

Environmental Education

Our outreach programs in schools and communities raise awareness about environmental issues and inspire the next generation of conservation leaders in Tanzania.
